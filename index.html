Why Labour Must Go

Small Heath deserves better

🗑️
Bin Strike Crisis
Streets swamped with rubbish and rats causing health problems including asthma attacks. The bin strike cost over £17 million — with fly-tipping completely out of control. Labour's poor leadership caused this and still hasn't resolved it.
🏚️
Inner City Neglect
Small Heath Pool closed for 10 years. Park House left to rot. Crime and anti-social behaviour on the rise. Heybarnes Fields and Digby Park received zero investment. Our community has been abandoned while budgets were squandered elsewhere.
💸
Financial Mismanagement
Council declared bankruptcy. Commonwealth Games costs spiralled from £560m to over £800m. Community assets sold cheaply. Essential library, youth and care services cut while commissioners are paid over £1,000 a day.
⚠️
Don't Trust Lib Dems Either
Collaborated with Labour to raise council tax to 24%. Went into coalition with Tories and let communities down. No investment in Small Heath when they held power. Tried and tested politicians who never delivered — don't waste your vote on them.
